About Faith Angels For Seniors

Faith Angel for Seniors is a Montego Bay–based nonprofit dedicated to uplifting and caring for vulnerable older adults. Whether living alone, in senior homes, or displaced by crisis, we ensure seniors receive dignity, compassion, and essential support.

Why Your Support Matters

Seniors are among the most at-risk during disasters. Many face overwhelming loss and uncertainty. Your contribution directly provides food, shelter, medical aid, and safe care for seniors with nowhere else to turn.

Your Gift at Work

Your gift can...

  • Serve a warm meal
  • Provide clean drinking water
  • Deliver medical & hygiene supplies
  • Create safe temporary shelter
  • Help rebuild damaged senior homes

Most Needed Items for Seniors

The devastation left by Hurricane Melissa means that hundreds of seniors lost their personal belongings and medical necessities in an instant. By donating or organizing a drive for the items below, you are helping us restore their dignity, comfort, and health.

Toiletries & Hygiene Essentials

  • Bath soaps/body wash (gentle, sensitive skin)
  • Deodorants ( gentle, roll-on preferred)
  • Shampoo & Conditioner
  • Body lotion
  • Toothbrush (soft bristle)
  • Toothpaste (sensitive option)
  • Mouthwash (alcohol free)
  • Denture cleaner & denture adhesive
  • Wet Wipes (fragrance free)
  • Hand Sanitizers
  • Washcloths
  • Tissues & paper Towels
  • Adult diapers (various sizes)
  • Incontinence pads/ bed liners
  • Barrier creams
  • Antibiotic Creams
  • Dettol
  • Vaseline/Petroleum jelly
  • Anti-fungal cream/powder
Hair & Grooming

  • Combs & brushes
  • Hair oil
  • Shaving cream
  • Razors (safety razors preferred)
  • Small mirror
Sanitary & Cleaning Items

  • Laundry detergent (sensitive skin)
  • Toilet paper
  • Paper Towell
  • Disinfectant wipes
  • Air fresheners (mild)
  • Surface cleaners
Clothing (Gently worn or New)
Small, Medium, Large

  • T-Shirts (men)
  • Undershirts ( Male & female)
  • Shorts (male)
  • Underwear (new – Male & Female)
  • Skirts
  • Summer Dresses
  • Bed wear ( male & female)
  • Slippers (Male & Female)
Beddings

  • Bed Sheets ( twin full and hospital bed sizes)
  • Pillowcases
  • Mattress protectors (waterproof)
  • Bed Pads (washable or disposable)
  • Pillows
Food and Nutrition

  • Bottled Water
  • Non-perishable foods (canned goods, crackers, cereals)
  • Meal Replacement drinks (Ensure)
Medical & Mobility Equipment
These items make the greatest impact for displaced or medically fragile seniors

  • Wheelchairs
  • Walkers
  • Canes
  • Hospital Beds
  • Portable Commodes
  • Shower Chairs & Bath Benches
  • Blood Pressure monitors
  • Glucose machines & test strips
  • First-aid kits
